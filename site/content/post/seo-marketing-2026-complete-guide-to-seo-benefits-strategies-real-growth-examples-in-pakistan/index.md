# **WHAT IS SEO MARKETING?**



SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of improving your website to rank #1 on Google when potential customers search for your products or services.

Example: When someone in Lahore searches "best dentist near me" and your clinic appears first, that's SEO. It means free traffic, free leads, and customers 24/7 without paying for ads.



## **5 KEY SEO ROLES THAT DRIVE BUISNESS GROWTH**



1. **Technical SEO:**  It focuses on improving website infrastructure, including page speed, mobile responsiveness, indexing, and crawlability. It also ensures that search engines can efficiently access, understand, and index the website, which ultimately improves visibility in search results.


2. **On-Page SEO:** This involves optimizing individual web pages through titles, headings, keyword placement, and overall content structure. It helps search engines accurately understand the relevance of each page and improves keyword ranking performance.


3. **Off-Page SEO:** This one focuses on external optimization factors such as backlinks, citations, and brand mentions across the web. It strengthens domain authority and trustworthiness, which positively influences search engine rankings.


4. **Content SEO:** It centers on creating high-quality, search-optimized content such as blogs, articles, guides, and FAQs. It generates long-term organic traffic and helps establish topical authority within the industry.


5. **Local SEO:** It optimizes an online presence for location-based searches, including Google Maps and “near me” queries. It increases local visibility and drives foot traffic, phone calls, and direct customer inquiries.
